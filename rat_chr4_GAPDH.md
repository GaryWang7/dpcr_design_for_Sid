SNVs can affect probe binding and should be avoided if possible. There are also repetitive elements in the repeat masker track. It is difficult to design probes that involve repetitive elements because there is a risk they will bind multiple regions in the genome with the same element.

We will mask repeats with N. Note how the sequence captures an area much larger than just the gene and is on the + strand. The get DNA in window is another tool that can retrieve sequences in target regions if you don't want all of the extra sequences. By convention, we design primers and probes using the + strand rather than the - strand.
