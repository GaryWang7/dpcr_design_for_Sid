This workflow describes how to create custom digital PCR primers targeting the promoter region OR exon 1 of human SRY

First examine SRY using the ucsc genome browser: https://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgTracks?db=hg38&lastVirtModeType=default&lastVirtModeExtraState=&virtModeType=default&virtMode=0&nonVirtPosition=&position=chrY%3A2786855%2D2787682&hgsid=1662373152_1DFFw2TBAhlHZq7mcWedJA5uZpN8
